What is Xenocide?

Xenocide is a single and multiplayer game where the player fights to survive an alien attack. You can choose to play singleplayer, where the rest of your team is AI (offline playing), or multiplayer, where your team are real people from around the globe. Our goal is to exceed players' expectations.

Xenocide is available to download for free on your PC, MAC, or Linux computer. With your first download, you get a week to try out Xenocide. After that you can choose to pay a one time price of $29.99, or pay 3 payments of $15 over 3 months.
